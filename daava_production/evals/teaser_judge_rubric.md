# LLM-as-a-Judge: Teaser Evaluation Rubric
**Project**: DAAVA | **Sequence**: Chapter One Teaser (Mirror Reality)

Use this rubric to audit the final render of the 15-second teaser against the **CDLC Managed Context**.

## 1. Visual DNA Continuity (Weight: 30%)
- [ ] **Character Lock**: Is the Orange Suit (#E67E22) consistent across space shots?
- [ ] **Wardrobe Match**: Does the Khadi match the established aesthetic for the Nampally Arjun?
- [ ] **Hardware Lock**: Is the deep blue harness (#2C3E50) visible in orbital scenes?

## 2. Mirror-Reality Choreography (Weight: 30%)
- [ ] **Match Cuts**: Do the cuts happen at established action points (gripping/looking)?
- [ ] **Dualism**: Is the contrast between space vacuum and rain-slicked Nampally clear?
- [ ] **Sync**: Do the transitions feel rhythmic (approx every 3 seconds)?

## 3. Cinematography Adherence (Weight: 20%)
- [ ] **Perspective**: Is the **Worm's-Eye View (Extreme Low)** dominant in Shot 1?
- [ ] **Scale**: Does the 14mm lens properly convey the scale of the tower vs. the void?
- [ ] **Lighting**: Is the warm golden rim light (#F39C12) vs. cyan neon (#00FFFF) contrast achieved?

## 4. Atmospheric Fidelity (Weight: 20%)
- [ ] **Brutalism**: Does the Kabutarkhana Tower feel gargantuan and concrete?
- [ ] **Noir Texture**: Are there rain reflections and smog-heavy dust hazes?

---
**Scoring:**
- **Cinematic Masterpiece**: 95% - 100%
- **Production Grade**: 85% - 94%
- **Reshoot Required**: < 85%
