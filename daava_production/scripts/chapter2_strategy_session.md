# Chapter Two: The Strategy Session

**STYLE**: Cyber-Brutalist / Industrial Noir
**LOCATION**: INT. KIRAN RAO'S OFFICE - BANJARA HILLS - DAY

### [SCENE START]

The office is a concrete cell perched above a mobile repair bazaar. A single window faces the smog-choked Banjara Hills skyline.

KIRAN RAO (40s), sharp eyes, moves like a man who knows the weight of every secret. He stands before a glass whiteboard, marking it with glowing teal indicators.

ARJUN VARMA (34) sits opposite him. He is still in his work gear—the industrial orange pressurized jumpsuit (#E67E22) and deep blue climbing harness (#2C3E50). He looks like he just stepped off the orbital pipeline.

A single shaft of warm golden light (#F39C12) pierces the dust motes, rim-lighting Arjun’s profile. From the floor below, the flickering cyan neon (#00FFFF) of the repair shops casts a jittery rhythm onto the ceiling.

                     KIRAN
           Tell me what you know.

Arjun places his weathered notebook on the table. The cover is scuffed, the corners frayed.

                     ARJUN
           Monday. Zafar Road. Pressure held 
           for forty-five minutes. On Tuesday, 
           it ran brown at 6:45.

Kiran scribbles "PRESSURE" on the glass. The teal ink glows against the gray concrete walls.

                     KIRAN
           You've been circling this for months, 
           Arjun. People don't want pressure 
           readings. They want a reason to hope.

                     ARJUN
           They're exhausted, Kiran. Not angry. 
           Exhausted. There's a difference.

Kiran stops writing. He turns. The 14mm lens perspective makes the small office feel both cavernous and suffocating.

                     KIRAN
           Exhausted people don't vote for change. 
           They vote for the devil they know.

Arjun grips the notebook. His knuckles are white.

                     ARJUN
           I want to contest. Nampally. 
           This cycle.

Kiran looks at Arjun for a long beat. The rim light catches the intensity in Arjun's eyes.

                     KIRAN
           (after a beat)
           Okay. I'm in. But you should know, 
           the climb is going to change what 
           you think you're fighting for.
