## ⚡ SEEDANCE 2.0 PRODUCTION PROMPT (READY-TO-PASTE)

AESTHETIC: Industrial Noir / Cyber-Brutalist. High contrast chiaroscuro. Warm golden rim light (#F39C12) vs flickering cyan neon (#00FFFF). Heavy dust haze and rain-slicked surfaces. Anamorphic 14mm ultra-wide lens. Muted earth tones (Khadi white, industrial grey) vs saturated safety orange (#E67E22).

STORY: Arjun Varma performs a high-stakes climb on an orbital pipeline, while simultaneously navigating the decaying streets of Nampally. Two worlds, one struggle.

CHARACTERS: Arjun from @image1. Athletic build. In space: Industrial orange suit (#E67E22) with deep blue harness (#2C3E50). In Nampally: Khadi shirt, weathered but clean.

ENVIRONMENT: Alternating between the vacuum-sealed orbital lattice truss against a gas giant and the rain-slicked, neon-lit streets of Nampally. Exposed digital veins (wiring) on brutalist concrete.

ACTION SEQUENCE:
Shot 1, 0–3s: Extreme low-angle Worm's-Eye View (14mm) — Arjun, in his orange suit (#E67E22), reaches for a rung on a vertical lattice truss. A massive gas giant glows in the background with a sharp golden rim light (#F39C12).
Shot 2, 3–6s: Match cut to street-level wide shot — Arjun, now in Khadi, walking through rain-slicked Nampally streets. Flickering cyan neons (#00FFFF) reflect in puddles. Brutalist Kabutarkhana tower looms in the smog.
Shot 3, 6–9s: Close-up on Arjun’s hands — in space, he grips a copper conduit; in Nampally, he grips his weathered notebook. Rapid match cut between the two.
Shot 4, 9–12s: Medium shot — Arjun stops in Nampally, looking up at a blue shirt on a clothesline. The lighting is a bruised indigo.
Shot 5, 12–15s: Wide drone pull-back — alternating between Arjun tiny against the void of space and Arjun tiny in the shadows of the massive concrete tower. Fade to black.

PRODUCTION BRIEF: 5 shots, 15 seconds. High shot density to create a rhythmic "Mirror" effect between the two locations. Match cuts on action (gripping, looking). Include audio: vacuum hum, rain on pavement, distant city sirens.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: No text overlays. No 35mm focal length in space scenes. No clean equipment. No bright daylight. No music.
