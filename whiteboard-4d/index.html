Draw in 3D spaceTap Draw, then drag anywhere — your stroke becomes a glowing 3D tube floating in space at the depth you started from.
🅣
Real 3D textTap 3D Text, click a spot, type — it appears as solid extruded lettering you can orbit around, not a flat label.
🎬
Images & live videoImage and Video place media on floating panels in the scene. Videos play and loop right on the board.
🟦
Place objects like MinecraftClick any surface — floor, back wall, or an existing object — and the new thing lands exactly on that face.
🔷
Shapes & stickersTap Shapes for 3D solids and 2D flats, or Stickers for emoji, animals & birds. Pick one from the palette, click a surface. In Nav mode, tap any object to make it bounce.
🦩
Living 3D crittersTap Critters for real animated 3D models — a flapping flamingo, parrot, stork or galloping horse. Pick one, click a surface, and watch it come to life.
🔗
Connect ideasTap Link, click two objects, and a bezier arc joins them. Build a 3D mind-map.
