Draw & Build in 3D

  • Draw — drag anywhere to paint glowing 3D tubes in space
  • 3D Text — click a spot, type, get solid extruded letters
  • Block — click any surface to stack voxels like Minecraft
  • Shapes — 12 solids + 8 flat shapes placed on any face
  • Stickers — 48 emoji & animals as floating sprites
🦩

Media & Living Objects

  • Image — drop any photo as a floating 3D panel
  • Video — plays & loops on the board (tap to pause)
  • Audio — spatial sound that fades with distance in 3D
  • Critters — flamingo, parrot, stork & horse with real animations
  • 4D toggle ⏱ — new objects drift through time like a tesseract
🚀

Move, Resize & Present

  • Move ✋ — drag to reposition · scroll = depth
  • Icons ⬡ — place tech (cloud, AI, server) & creative (camera, VFX, edit) nodes with pulsing rings
  • Link 🔗 — click two objects → animated particle flows A→B · right-click link to reverse direction
  • Draw ✏️ — finish a stroke to get suggestions: convert to text, image, or icon node
  • 🔴 Record — capture the board + mic + webcam picture-in-picture
