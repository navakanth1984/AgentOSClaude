History & Geography Revision
Q. What is the meaning of the word 'Upanishad'?
Ans: The word 'Upanishad' means **"to sit down beside"**. They are a compilation of teachings that were orally transmitted to students.
Q. What is a Map? Name its three components.
Ans: A **Map** is a two-dimensional representation of the earth, or parts of it, on a flat surface. The three components of a map are **Scale, Direction, and Symbols**.
Q. What are the four cardinal directions?
Ans: The four main directions are **North (N), South (S), East (E), and West (W)**. They are known as cardinal directions.
Q. Who is a Tirthankara? What does 'Ahimsa' mean?
Ans: A **Tirthankara** is a person who has achieved enlightenment and shows the path to others. **Ahimsa** means non-violence, involving abstinence from killing or injuring other living beings.
