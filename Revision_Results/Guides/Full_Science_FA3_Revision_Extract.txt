How can you measure the length of
a curved line? Describe one method
in detail?

A curved line cannot be measured
using a meter scale A thread or a
divider should be used for this
measurement.

Open out a divider so that the
legs are about 5mm a part. Place
one of the legs at one end of
the line and the other leg on
the line. With out altering the
distance between the legs of the
divider, take the divider along
the line. Count the number of steps
taken to cover the entire line. If some
portion is left out, measure it separately.
The length of the curved line is calculated
as Length = number of steps x distance between
legs + length of the left over line.

What is the difference between circular
and oscillatory motion? Illustrate with
examples -

Circular motion :-
When the motion of an object is around
a fixed point that is outside the
object, it is called circular motion.
The motion of a stone tied to a
string and whirled in a circle, and
the motion of the Earth around
the sun are examples of circular motion.

Oscillatory motion:-
When a body moves to - and - fro
about a fixed point it is said to
be in oscillatory motion or vibratory
motion. The motion of a pendulum
and a vibrating string are
examples for oscillatory motion.
