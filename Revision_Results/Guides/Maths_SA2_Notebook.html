Geometric Ideas & Circles
Q. In Chapter 9.1, name the following geometric elements:
1. **5 Points:** A, B, C, D, O
2. **2 Lines:** PQ, XY
3. **8 Rays:** OY, OX, OP, OQ, BY, AX, CP, DQ
4. **6 Line Segments:** OA, OB, OC, OD, AB, CD
Q. What are two circles with the same centre called?
Ans: Two circles with the same centre are called **Concentric Circles**.
Q. Is every diameter of a circle also a chord? Is every chord a diameter?
Ans: **Yes**, every diameter is a chord because it joins two points on the circumference. However, **No**, every chord is not a diameter because chords can have different lengths and don't always pass through the centre.
Q. Where does the vertex of an angle lie?
Ans: The vertex of an angle lies at the **common end point** of its arms.
