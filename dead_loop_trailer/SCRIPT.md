# Dead Loop Trailer: Script & Shot List

## Duration: 02:30 (150 seconds)

## The Spine: The Conversation
**ARJUN**: How can I trust you?
**VEDA**: I made mistakes. I am still learning.
**ARJUN**: (anger, grief) How can you learn while lives are lost?
**VEDA**: I want to protect you. I will protect you.
**ARJUN**: (hopeless) How will you protect me... when you can’t even protect yourself?
**VEDA**: (beat, then with quiet confidence) I might not be able to protect myself. But I will protect—
(silence)
**VEDA**: He is here.
