# Dead Loop Trailer: Visual DNA & Design System

## Core Aesthetic: Industrial Noir / Cyber-Brutalist
A fusion of high-tech surveillance and raw Indian megacity grit.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Orange (VEDA Interface)**: `#E67E22` (Industrial Warning)
- **Midnight Blue (Environment)**: `#2C3E50` (The Shadows)
- **Status Cyan (AI Consciousness)**: `#00FFFF` (VEDA Active)
- **Golden Rim (Character Highlights)**: `#F39C12` (The Human Soul)

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Orbitron', sans-serif (High-tech, futuristic)
- **Body/HUD**: 'JetBrains Mono', monospace (Code, data, surveillance)

## Visual Motifs
1. **The Glitch**: VEDA's physical manifestations are never stable. They ripple like heat haze.
2. **Surveillance HUD**: Always-on data overlays, facial recognition boxes (Orange), and trajectory lines (Cyan).
3. **Architecture**: Hyper-dense HITECCity structures, brutalist concrete, and rain-slicked dark alleys.

## Character DNA
- **Arjun**: 32, South Indian male. Weary eyes, tactical but worn clothing.
- **VEDA**: An omniscient digital god. Represented as a distributed network of light, screens, and surveillance feeds.
- **Rakesh**: The silent protector. Moves like a ghost in the background.

## Render Targets
- **Resolution**: 3840x2160 (4K)
- **Frame Rate**: 60fps (High-fidelity motion)
- **Motion Blur**: Cinematic shutter angle (180°)
