4Q. What is weaving?

II. Long Questions

1G. Name five functions of vitamins.

Ans: Vitamins are a group of nutrients that our body needs in small quantities. They are essential for:

1. Vitamin A: Vision and skin health.
2. Vitamin B: Energy and nervous system.
3. Vitamin C: Gums, joints and immunity.
4. Vitamin D: Bones and teeth.
5. Vitamin E: Healthy skin and hair.
