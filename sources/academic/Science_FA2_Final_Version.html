II. Long Questions

1G. Name five functions of vitamins.

Ans: Vitamins are essential for various body functions:

  1. Vitamin A: Keeps eyes and skin healthy.
  2. Vitamin B: Helps in energy production.
  3. Vitamin C: Helps body fight against diseases (Immunity).
  4. Vitamin D: Helps in bone and tooth health.
  5. Vitamin E: Necessary for healthy skin and muscles.
