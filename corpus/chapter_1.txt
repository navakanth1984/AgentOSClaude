Chapter 1: The Awakening

The sun rose over the desolate wasteland. "This is not what I expected," muttered John, kicking a rusted can across the cracked pavement. 

Sarah adjusted her backpack and sighed. "We knew the risks when we left the vault. The radiation outside is mostly gone, but the scavengers aren't."

"Do you think they're watching us right now?" John asked, glancing nervously at the ruined buildings towering above them like skeletal fingers.

"Always," she replied, her voice cold and even. "Keep your weapon ready, and don't stray too far."

Suddenly, a loud crash echoed from the alleyway to their left. Both of them froze.

"What was that?" John whispered.

"Only one way to find out," Sarah said, drawing her pistol.
