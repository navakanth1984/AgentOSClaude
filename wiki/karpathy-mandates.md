# Karpathy Foundation Mandates
> Four execution principles that govern all AI work in this workspace.

## Summary
A short charter of mental models that override default agent behavior to favor efficiency and surgical precision.

## Details
1. **Think before coding** — fully understand the problem, plan the architecture, and form explicit hypotheses before writing or modifying anything.
2. **Prioritize simplicity** — seek the most direct, readable solution; complexity is a liability. If it fits in 10 lines, don't write 100.
3. **Execute surgical changes** — touch only what must change; don't reformat or refactor unrelated code; preserve existing structure and behavior.
4. **Goal-driven execution** — every action must serve the stated objective; verify alignment continuously; avoid tangents unless they become blockers.
