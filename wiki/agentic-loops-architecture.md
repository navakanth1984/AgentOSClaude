# Agentic Loops Architecture
> Blueprint for self-updating agents, context life cycles, and Obsidian second-brain syncs.

## Summary
The umbrella theme tying together this workspace's AI-systems work: memory-state triggers, RAG ingestion, type-safe CLI routines, and the "software 3.0" idea that the knowledge base maintains itself. Three concrete architectures sit under it, each now with its own page.

## Details
- **Knowledge ingestion / RAG** — managed-cloud approach in [Nth Dimension Academy](nth-dimension-academy.md); structured-data-to-Markdown approach in [OKF Bundle Generator](okf-bundle-generator.md).
- **External tool bridging** — [NotebookLM Bridge](notebooklm-bridge.md) for pulling context from services without an official API.
- **Operating discipline** — the [Karpathy Mandates](karpathy-mandates.md) govern how every agent acts.
- **Memory loops** — `memory_os/` and the Obsidian vault provide cross-session state; this wiki is itself an instance of the self-improving loop.
