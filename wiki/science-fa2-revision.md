# Science FA2 Revision
> Study and revision material for the Class Science FA2 (Formative Assessment 2) cycle.

## Summary
A set of HTML revision documents for Science FA2, produced in several iterations as the layout and content fidelity were refined. Bilingual academic content (English plus a Telugu edition) sits alongside it.

## Details
- Multiple versions exist, tracking the revision's evolution: Comprehensive, Distillation, High Fidelity, Fidelity Final, Final Version, Horizontal Page, and a "Stable" build.
- Companion packaged outputs: `science_fa2_output.zip` and `science_fa2_te_output.zip` (Telugu edition).
- A related Social FA2 revision image is filed in the same folder.
- Sources (in `sources/academic/`): `Science_FA2_Final_Version.html`, `Science_Revision_Stable.html`, and the other version variants.
- Live extraction working folders remain at the repo root: `science_fa2_extracted/`, `science_fa2_te_extracted/`, `science_fa3_extracted/`.

## Connections
- Part of the academic content stream that also feeds the teaching agent in [Nth Dimension Academy](nth-dimension-academy.md).
