# DP-700 Atlas — Neural Map (MOC)

> Master map for **Exam DP-700: Implementing Data Engineering Solutions Using Microsoft Fabric**.
> Open Obsidian **Graph View** to see the neural network. Domains → Skill Groups → Concept Neurons.

## How this map works
- **3 Domains** (each 30–35% of the exam) are the lobes.
- **10 Skill Groups** are the exam's measured objectives.
- **12 Concept Neurons** are the cross-cutting Fabric technologies. The same neuron (e.g. [[OneLake]], [[Spark]]) fires across multiple domains — those shared links are what make this a *map* and not a *tree*.

## The three lobes
- [[Domain 1 — Implement & Manage]]  *(Implement and manage an analytics solution)*
- [[Domain 2 — Ingest & Transform]]  *(Ingest and transform data)*
- [[Domain 3 — Monitor & Optimize]]  *(Monitor and optimize an analytics solution)*
