# CDLC Presentation Design System

## Style Prompt
A high-fidelity, technical blueprint aesthetic. Minimalist dark interface with glowing cyan circuitry and slate-gray structural elements. The design feels like an advanced engineering terminal, using precise lines, technical callouts, and smooth, purposeful motion to represent the "Context Development Life Cycle."

## Colors
- **Background**: `#0A0A0A` (Deep Space)
- **Primary**: `#00E5FF` (Context Flow Cyan)
- **Secondary**: `#2C3E50` (Slate Structural)
- **Accent**: `#FF9100` (Legacy Warning Orange)
- **Text High**: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)
- **Text Med**: `#B0B0B0` (Silver Gray)

## Typography
- **Headlines**: 'Outfit', sans-serif (Weight: 700)
- **Body/Labels**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Weight: 400, 500)
- **Monospace**: 'JetBrains Mono', monospace (For technical callouts)

## Motion Rules
- **Entrance**: Staggered `expo.out` for titles. Draw-in effect for "Context Flow" paths.
- **Transitions**: "Flow Wipe" - a cyan light sweep that carries data from one scene to the next.
- **Ambient**: Subtle pulse on the "Engine" core and drifting particles in the background.

## What NOT to Do
- Avoid jump cuts.
- Do not use generic blue (#3b82f6).
- No rounded corners on structural elements (keep it sharp and engineered).
- Avoid cluttered layouts; prioritize white space (negative space).
