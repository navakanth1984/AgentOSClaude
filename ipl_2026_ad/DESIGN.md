## Style Prompt
An electrifying, high-energy, cinematic stadium aesthetic for the Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026. The mood is explosive, fast-paced, and stadium-lit, featuring intense glowing neon accents against a deep, dark pitch-black background to emulate night matches under floodlights.

## Colors
- `--bg-dark`: `#0B0C10` (Deep Stadium Night)
- `--accent-blue`: `#1A53D8` (IPL Blue - Glow and secondary text)
- `--accent-gold`: `#F4C430` (Trophy Gold - For primary impact text)
- `--accent-red`: `#E63946` (Cricket Ball Red - For bursts and warnings)
- `--text-main`: `#FFFFFF` (Pure white for readability)

## Typography
- Primary: `Rajdhani`, sans-serif (Geometric, technical, sporty)
- Secondary: `Inter`, sans-serif (Clean data and dates)

## What NOT to Do
- NO generic flat backgrounds (use radial gradients or glow).
- NO slow, languid fades. Easing must be aggressive and springy.
- NO unstyled white text on black. Always add a drop shadow or glow.
