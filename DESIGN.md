# DESIGN: Blade Runner 2049 (Server Room)

## Style Prompt
Vast, oppressive darkness punctuated by precise, high-contrast light. A "cathedral of servers" where the environment is felt through silhouette and atmospheric mist rather than direct lighting. The aesthetic is clean but weathered, focusing on the rhythmic "heartbeat" of the machines.

## Colors
- **Primary Darkness:** `#020406` (Deepest indigo-black)
- **LED Cyan:** `#00f3ff` (High-intensity cyan)
- **Mist Glow:** `rgba(0, 243, 255, 0.05)` (Subtle atmospheric diffusion)
- **Shadow Texture:** `#0a0c0e` (Slightly lifted black for depth)

## Typography
- **Monospaced Technical:** `JetBrains Mono` or `Courier Prime` (Small, precise metadata if needed)
- **Display:** `Inter` (Thin weight, widely spaced)

## Motion Rules
- **Pulsing:** High-energy attack, slow decay. The pulses should feel like a heartbeat — organic but precise.
- **Mist:** Extremely slow, drift-like movement.
- **Timing:** Respect the silence. 3 seconds of total blackness before the machine "wakes up."

## What NOT to Do
- No flat backgrounds; use subtle radial gradients or grain to avoid H.264 banding in the darkness.
- No saturated colors other than the Cyan.
- No fast camera movement; keep the frame static and heavy.
